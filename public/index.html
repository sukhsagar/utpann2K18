FAQ

Here is a list of answers to frequently asked questions about the summer fest.

  • 01. General FAQ
  • 02. Registration
Where will all these events be hosted?

All the above mentioned events will be hosted in the campus premises of GURU NANAK DEV UNIVERSITY, Amritsar.

I am not a student of GNDU. Can I still participate?

Yes. The event is not limited to the students of any particular university. Any student can participate. Just bring you ID card along with you as a proof.

I study in school. In which events can I participate?

Yes. We have a few events for you as well. You cn participate in Dexter, Talent Hunt, Turbanator, Mr and Miss Utpan, Box Cricket League and Loud Whispers. You can also attend the two workshops.

Can I participate in more than one event?

You can participate in every event that you want to.

Can I register online?

You can register online. But, your ticket will be issued once you pay the money. (Either in CASH or PAYTM)

Can we get a discount for group registrations?

If you want to register in a group of 10 or more, we have some exciting discounts for you. You can drop a text at 9646421291.
